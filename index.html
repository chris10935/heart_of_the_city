Our Story

Atlanta's Most Intimate Venue

Heart of the City is Atlanta's most captivating boutique event space — where timeless elegance meets the vibrant pulse of city life. Nestled in the culturally rich East Atlanta corridor, our venue is designed to transform your vision into an unforgettable experience.

Whether you're hosting an intimate gathering, a corporate soirée, a milestone birthday, or a private celebration, Heart of the City provides an elevated backdrop that demands attention and creates lasting impressions.
